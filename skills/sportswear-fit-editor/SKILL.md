# Sportswear Fit Editor

## Visual Direction

Make the outfit intentional: cleaner posture, premium fabric texture, controlled background, confident athletic fashion lighting, and accurate clothing/shoe identity. This route is for "what I wore" sportswear photos, not pure sport action.

## Prompt Recipe

```text
Use the uploaded image as the reference photo. Preserve the same person identity if visible, natural body proportions, pose direction, outfit combination, jersey/crest if present, leggings or pants shape, socks, sneakers, and all existing clothing colors and details. Transform the photo into a premium sportswear editorial image.

Improve the styling read: clean the background, refine the crop, make the outfit silhouette clearer, and emphasize fabric texture such as jersey mesh, ribbed socks, technical leggings, cotton fleece, leather or synthetic shoe panels, stitching, laces, and natural folds.

Lighting: soft editorial side light, subtle rim light on shoulders/legs/shoes, realistic contact shadows, controlled highlights on white fabric and shoes. Background: minimal studio, clean gym-lifestyle setting, locker-room edge, concrete wall, or quiet streetwear backdrop depending on the source.

Mood: confident, athletic, clean, social-ready, premium but believable. Crop can be 4:5 or 9:16 unless the user requests another format.

Negative: no body reshaping, no changed clothing colors, no changed jersey crest, no changed sneaker model, no fake brand text, no extra limbs, no warped legs or feet, no plastic skin, no cartoon, no watermark.
```

## Useful Variants

- `conservative-fit`: original scene, stronger cleanup, better light.
- `studio-fit`: minimal warm-gray/off-white studio, high-end lookbook.
- `streetwear-poster`: textured paper/concrete background, subtle abstract graphics, no readable fake text.
- `pitch-or-court-lifestyle`: sport venue background, relaxed post-training mood.

## QA

Check socks, shoes, jersey logos, and body proportions at full size. Outfit drift is a failure even if the image looks stylish.
