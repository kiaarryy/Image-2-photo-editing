# Sports Action Editor

## Visual Direction

Dynamic sports editorial with believable venue cues, crisp subject separation, realistic sweat/fabric detail, and movement energy. Preserve anatomy and actual sport context more aggressively than graphic drama.

## Prompt Recipe

```text
Use the uploaded image as the reference photo. Preserve the same person identity if visible, natural body proportions, pose direction, sport, outfit, shoes, jersey details, and existing logos/crests. Transform the photo into a high-end sports editorial image with realistic athletic energy.

Improve the action read: cleaner background, stronger subject separation, sharper fabric and shoe texture, realistic sweat or exertion only if appropriate, and clear court/field/gym context. Add controlled sports lighting: directional key light, subtle rim light, clean shadows, and natural highlights on skin and technical fabric.

If the source has motion, keep motion blur directional and believable. If the source is static, add energy through composition, light, and environment rather than fake body movement.

Color grade: punchy but realistic, protected skin tones, whites with texture, deep but readable blacks, venue colors kept plausible.

Negative: no impossible pose, no extra limbs, no distorted hands/feet, no changed shoe or jersey design, no fake sponsor text, no random crowd chaos, no plastic skin, no cartoon, no watermark.
```

## Sport Contexts

- Football/basketball/court: preserve jersey/crest, add court/field lines only if plausible.
- Gym/training: keep equipment realistic, avoid fake brand signage.
- Running/outdoor: preserve stride and body mechanics; use natural rim light and atmospheric depth.

## QA

Reject edits where motion energy creates anatomy errors or changes the sport/outfit identity.
