# Style Archetypes

Use these as reusable social-media photography recipes. They are inspired by public creator/tutorial patterns, not exact living-person mimicry.

## creator-clean-retouch

For ordinary photos that should look professionally edited but not transformed. Preserve composition, correct color cast, reduce noise, sharpen real detail, clean small distractions, and keep skin/fabric realistic.

## warm-travel-editorial

For travel and daily-life portraits. Natural warm light, architectural framing, calm background, realistic place atmosphere, gentle color, no HDR tourism look.

## cinematic-social-portrait

For city, night, cafe, car, mirror, and expressive social portraits. Directional light, subject separation, filmic contrast, warm highlights, cooler shadows, controlled saturation, no fake neon text.

## fashion-portrait-retouch

For portrait/fashion images. Subtle RAW-style global adjustments, HSL color control, clean skin texture, natural face identity, polished styling, no plastic beauty filter.

## sports-editorial-action

For gym, running, court, field, and training photos. Crisp athletic texture, readable motion, venue realism, rim light, punchy contrast, no impossible anatomy.

## streetwear-sports-fit

For jerseys, leggings, socks, sneakers, and outfit photos. Make styling intentional with cleaner posture, premium fabric texture, minimal studio/gym/streetwear background, and accurate outfit identity.

## commercial-sneaker-hero

For sneaker and product detail images. Product-first composition, accurate shoe model/colorway, large-softbox lighting, crisp contact shadows, visible stitching/laces/mesh/rubber texture, clean campaign finish.

## dynamic-product-campaign

For product images meant to feel more like a social ad. Keep product accuracy, add controlled diagonal composition, paper/concrete/athletic texture, subtle abstract motion graphics, and no readable fake text.
