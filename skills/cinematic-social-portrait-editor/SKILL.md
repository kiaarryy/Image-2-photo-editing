# Cinematic Social Portrait Editor

## Visual Direction

Clean social-media portrait with cinematic contrast, shaped light, clear subject separation, controlled shadows, and strong but believable color. Useful for urban, night, street, mirror, cafe, car, subway, and casual portrait images.

## Prompt Recipe

```text
Use the uploaded image as the reference photo. Preserve the same person identity, facial structure if visible, body proportions, pose direction, outfit, accessories, and location logic. Turn the photo into a cinematic social-media portrait with strong subject presence and realistic editorial polish.

Lighting: controlled directional light on the face/body, soft falloff, subtle rim light if plausible, clean catchlights if eyes are visible, and preserved skin texture. Color grade: filmic contrast, slightly lifted or clean blacks depending on the source, warm highlights, cooler shadows or muted city tones, restrained saturation, no neon overload.

Composition: simplify the background, remove visual clutter, keep enough negative space for a feed cover or 4:5 portrait crop, and guide attention to the face/outfit without making the image look artificial.

Negative: no face identity drift, no plastic skin, no heavy face reshaping, no fake signs or random readable text, no over-dark crushed shadows, no extra limbs, no warped hands, no watermark.
```

## Variants

- `daylight-feed`: softer contrast, clean warm highlights, natural skin.
- `night-city`: deeper shadows, controlled practical lights, gentle halation, no fake neon signs.
- `profile-cover`: more negative space, cleaner background, less texture/noise.

## QA

The result should look like a strong edited portrait from the same shoot, not a movie poster with a different person.
