## Translation Into This Project

- Travel/lifestyle portraits: natural light, architectural framing, place atmosphere, clean negative space.
- Social/cinematic portraits: controlled contrast, moody shadows, filmic highlights, less clutter, expressive but realistic skin.
- Sports/action: dynamic pose, crisp fabric/sweat texture, venue cues, rim light, motion energy without distorted anatomy.
- Sportswear outfit: preserve outfit identity; make styling intentional through lighting, posture, background, and texture.
- Sneaker/product: product-first lighting, accurate shoe shape, realistic material panels, controlled shadows, clean background.
